# Indonesian CPO Company Profiles

> **Created:** 2026-03-11
> **Coverage:** 25 Actively Listed Indonesian CPO Companies (Excludes suspended: GOLL, MAGP, JAWA)
> **Parent Deep Dive:** [Crude Palm Oil (CPO)](crude-palm-oil.md)

This document serves as a companion to the main CPO industry primer. It provides specific profiles for publicly listed Indonesian palm oil companies, categorizing their scale, economic moats, differentiation, and crucially, their plantation age structure — the primary determinant of future yield and CapEx cycles.

*Note: Plantation age structure generalized as Young (<7 years), Prime (8–18 years), or Old/Aging (>18 years).*

---

### Tier 1: Integrated Giants & Conglomerate Arms

#### 1. SMAR - PT Sinar Mas Agro Resources and Technology Tbk
- **Profile:** The flagship Indonesian entity of Golden Agri-Resources (GAR), part of the massive Sinar Mas Group. One of the largest integrated palm oil companies in the world.
- **Economic Moat:** **Massive Scale & Downstream Value Add.** Controls the entire value chain from vast landbanks to refining, oleochemicals, and consumer cooking oil brands (Filma, Kunci Mas). High resilience to raw CPO price crashes due to downstream margin capture.
- **Differentiation:** Unmatched global distribution network and sheer scale of downstream refinery capacity.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Mature/Prime.** Average age is in the prime yielding bracket (~17–19 years). Generates massive cash flow but will require steady replanting CapEx over the next 5-10 years.

#### 2. AALI - PT Astra Agro Lestari Tbk
- **Profile:** The agribusiness arm of PT Astra International Tbk (the largest conglomerate in Indonesia). Historically considered a blue-chip upstream CPO player.
- **Economic Moat:** **Operational Excellence & Conglomerate Backing.** Benefits from the "Astra Management System," resulting in extremely efficient mill operations and agronomy practices. Strong balance sheet.
- **Differentiation:** Traditionally an upstream pure-play that has slowly added downstream capacity. Renowned for transparent corporate governance and ESG reporting.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Aging.** Aali has one of the older age profiles among the big players (average >16 years). It is currently undergoing an accelerated replanting program, which suppresses short-term volume growth but ensures long-term sustainability.

#### 3. SIMP - PT Salim Ivomas Pratama Tbk
- **Profile:** The vertically integrated agribusiness arm of the Salim Group (Indofood) and the parent company of LSIP.
- **Economic Moat:** **Captive Internal Demand.** Its ultimate parent produces Indomie (which requires massive amounts of palm oil). SIMP supplies the internal ecosystem and produces leading consumer cooking oil brand "Bimoli." 
- **Differentiation:** Vertical integration directly into Indonesia's largest FMCG conglomerate protects demand and secures margins.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Mature/Prime.** Generally sits in the prime age bracket, though with variation across its vast estates.

#### 4. LSIP - PT PP London Sumatra Indonesia Tbk
- **Profile:** A subsidiary of SIMP (Salim Group). One of Indonesia's oldest plantation companies.
- **Economic Moat:** **Premium Seed Genetics & Zero Debt.** Produces the renowned "Sumatra Bioscience" premium palm seeds. Famously conservative, operating typically with zero debt and a cash-rich balance sheet.
- **Differentiation:** Highly profitable upstream pure-play. Does not engage heavily in downstream refining, relying on its parent (SIMP) for integration.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Mature/Aging.** Has a mix of prime and older trees (~16-17 years avg), necessitating steady replanting, funded entirely through internal cash.

### Tier 2: Large & Mid-Cap (Pure-Play & Semi-Integrated)

#### 5. TAPG - PT Triputra Agro Persada Tbk
- **Profile:** Founded by the Rachmat family (ex-Astra). A highly efficient, rapidly growing upstream player.
- **Economic Moat:** **Best-in-Class Tree Age Profile.** TAPG boasts one of the youngest and most productive plantation age profiles in the industry, guaranteeing years of volume growth without heavy replanting CapEx.
- **Differentiation:** Incredible focus on operational agronomy and precision technology, mimicking Astra's efficiency but with younger, better-yielding biological assets.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Young/Prime.** Average age is around 12–13 years — entering the peak of the prime yielding curve. Structurally positioned for massive free cash flow generation.

#### 6. DSNG - PT Dharma Satya Nusantara Tbk
- **Profile:** Established CPO player with secondary operations in engineered wood products.
- **Economic Moat:** **ESG Premium & Yield Optimization.** DSNG is recognized for strict RSPO/ISPO adherence and efficient operations in East Kalimantan. Early adopter of methane capture facilities to lower carbon footprint.
- **Differentiation:** Strong ESG credentials allow it to sell at premiums or maintain access to strict Western/international buyers.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Prime.** Average age ~13 years. Favorable profile supporting continued high FFB yields and strong cash generation.

#### 7. SSMS - PT Sawit Sumbermas Sarana Tbk
- **Profile:** A Central Kalimantan-focused CPO producer owned by the Citra Borneo Indah Group.
- **Economic Moat:** **Concentrated Location & River Logistics.** Having all estates clustered in Central Kalimantan allows for hyper-efficient centralized milling and cheap barging logistics down river systems.
- **Differentiation:** Historically boasts some of the highest FFB yields and Oil Extraction Rates (OER) in the industry due to excellent soil matching and concentrated management.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Prime.** Mid-teens average age profile (around 14 years), meaning it is in the sweet spot of production but will need to plan for eventual replanting.

#### 8. TBLA - PT Tunas Baru Lampung Tbk
- **Profile:** Part of the Sungai Budi Group (Rose Brand). A major player in both sugar and palm oil.
- **Economic Moat:** **Sugar-Palm Diversification & Consumer Brands.** Unique dual-commodity structure mitigates CPO price volatility. Dominates the domestic cooking oil and sugar retail markets under the "Rose Brand" label.
- **Differentiation:** Very aggressive in downstream integration (biodiesel and oleochemicals) compared to its size.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Prime.** Stable tree age, producing consistent upstream cash flows to fund its heavy downstream/sugar expansions.

#### 9. STAA - PT Sumber Tani Agung Resources Tbk
- **Profile:** North Sumatra-based player with deep roots in the original heartland of Indonesian palm oil.
- **Economic Moat:** **Geographical Cluster Efficiency.** Operates tightly clustered, highly productive estates in North/South Sumatra with excellent logistical access to major export ports.
- **Differentiation:** A conservative, family-backed operator that runs very tight, high-margin upstream operations.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Prime.** Average age is strong (around 13-14 years), providing a solid runway for cash generation without an immediate CapEx burden for replanting.

#### 10. TLDN - PT Teladan Prima Agro Tbk
- **Profile:** pure-play upstream and midstream palm oil company focused entirely in East Kalimantan.
- **Economic Moat:** **Logistical Synergy.** Operations are strategically close to East Kalimantan's growing infrastructure hubs (and the new capital, IKN), providing logistical cost advantages.
- **Differentiation:** Dedicated regional focus ensures management is extremely dialed into local agronomy and regulatory conditions.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Prime.** Solid maturity profile ensuring stable FFB output over the next five years.

#### 11. BWPT - PT Eagle High Plantations Tbk
- **Profile:** Controlled by the Rajawali Group, previously faced severe financial stress but is in the midst of a turnaround.
- **Economic Moat:** **Massive Landbank Size.** Possesses a huge total landbank, though historically struggled to optimize yield from it.
- **Differentiation:** A turnaround play focused on deleveraging, improving agronomy, and increasing mill utilization after years of underperformance.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Aging/Mixed.** Historically had issues with delayed replanting during financial distress; now working to fix its age profile, requiring significant capital.

#### 12. SGRO - PT Sampoerna Agro Tbk
- **Profile:** Palm and sago company backed by the Sampoerna family.
- **Economic Moat:** **Premium Seed Production.** Like LSIP, SGRO produces superior palm seeds ("DxP Sriwijaya"), creating an additional high-margin revenue stream and ensuring their own estates have top-tier genetics.
- **Differentiation:** Diversified into sago production, and heavily supports plasma (smallholder) farmers.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Mature/Prime.** A balanced profile, effectively managing replanting cycles without shocking overall production volumes.

### Tier 3: Smaller & Niche Upstream Players

#### 13. CSRA - PT Cisadane Sawit Raya Tbk
- **Profile:** Smaller, highly efficient upstream player operating primarily in North Sumatra and South Sumatra.
- **Economic Moat:** **Legacy North Sumatra Yields.** Excellent soil and legacy agronomic practices in North Sumatra result in high FFB yields per hectare.
- **Differentiation:** Very disciplined debt management and gradual, sustainable expansion through targeted acquisitions rather than massive greenfield clearing.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Young-to-Prime.** Expanding strategically ensures a healthy blending of younger trees coming into yield.

#### 14. FAPA - PT FAP Agri Tbk
- **Profile:** An upstream palm oil producer with operations primarily in North Kalimantan, East Kalimantan, and Riau.
- **Economic Moat:** **Untapped Landbank.** Operates in newer frontiers (North Kalimantan) where land is cheaper, though logistics are tougher.
- **Differentiation:** Focused on aggressively expanding planted hectares and raising mill capacities.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Young.** A significant portion of its trees are young, meaning it is positioned for volume growth as these trees mature into prime yielders.

#### 15. GZCO - PT Gozco Plantations Tbk
- **Profile:** Small-cap player focused in South Sumatra and Central Kalimantan.
- **Differentiation:** Pure upstream play, highly leveraged to spot CPO price movements. Historically volatile financial performance.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Prime/Aging.**

#### 16. MGRO - PT Mahkota Group Tbk
- **Profile:** Historically a mid-stream player (owning CPO mills without their own plantations), now integrating.
- **Differentiation:** Evolving from a pure processor (buying FFB from third parties) into an integrated player by acquiring plantations and building refining/biodiesel capacity.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **N/A to Young.** Its core business historically relied on third-party fruit, but newly acquired plantations are young/developing.

#### 17. PALM - PT Provident Investasi Bersama Tbk
- **Profile:** Backed by Saratoga and Provident Group. *Crucial Note: PALM has pivoted its business model.*
- **Differentiation:** No longer a pure CPO operator. It has divested its CPO assets to become a broad-based investment holding company, though historically known for palm.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **N/A** (Transitioned away from direct plantation operations).

#### 18. PGUN - PT Pradiksi Gunatama Tbk
- **Profile:** Integrated palm oil firm located in Paser, East Kalimantan.
- **Differentiation:** Merged with identically owned Senabangun Anekaperti (Sena) giving it a neatly consolidated, single-location operation in East Kalimantan.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Young/Prime.** Reaps benefits of new planting cycles coming into maturity.

#### 19. PNGO - PT Pinago Utama Tbk
- **Profile:** Agribusiness operator in South Sumatra dealing in both palm oil and natural rubber.
- **Differentiation:** Dual commodity (Rubber/CPO) provides some hedge, though margins are structurally lower in rubber compared to prime palm.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Aging/Mature.**

#### 20. PTPS - PT Pulau Subur Tbk
- **Profile:** A very small, newly listed (2023) CPO player operating in South Sumatra.
- **Differentiation:** Micro-cap pure upstream play using IPO funds to build its first PKS (palm oil mill).
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Prime.**

#### 21. PSGO - PT Palma Serasih Tbk
- **Profile:** Upstream and midstream operator focused in East Kalimantan.
- **Differentiation:** Aggressively expanding its planted area and mill capacity in a targeted regional cluster to achieve economies of scale.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Very Young.** A massive portion of its plantations are young or immature, meaning negative short-term cash flow but tremendous volume growth potential in 3-5 years.

#### 22. ANDI - PT Andira Agro Tbk
- **Profile:** A micro-cap South Sumatra-based CPO producer.
- **Differentiation:** Pure upstream and milling player, highly reliant on third-party FFB sourcing to feed its mills due to limited nucleus landbank.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Mixed / Heavy reliance on external inputs.**

#### 23. CBUT - PT Citra Borneo Utama Tbk
- **Profile:** The downstream refining arm split from the Citra Borneo Indah Group (sister company to SSMS).
- **Differentiation:** Pure downstream play (refinery, oleochemicals). It buys CPO (mostly from SSMS) and value-adds. Shielded from biological upstream risks.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **N/A** (Downstream Refinery).

#### 24. JARR - PT Jhonlin Agro Raya Tbk
- **Profile:** Belonging to the Jhonlin Group (Haji Isam), based in South Kalimantan.
- **Differentiation:** Highly focused on the downstream biodiesel (FAME) mandate. It operates a massive biodiesel plant feeding directly into the government's B35/B40 programs.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Young / Developing.** Heavily focused downstream over its own upstream assets.

#### 25. MKTR - PT Menthobi Karyatama Raya Tbk
- **Profile:** Small-cap company with plantations and a mill in Central Kalimantan.
- **Differentiation:** Focuses heavily on waste management integration (turning POME into fertilizer and value-added products) as an efficiency driver.
- **Plantation Age Structure:** **Young to Prime.**
